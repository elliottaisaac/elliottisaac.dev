Commitment
to Quality

Biopure® is committed to growth and development to ensure the delivery of the highest quality nutraceutical products. We strive to provide supplements backed by science and focused on ingredients that benefit our customers' health.

Our ingredients are tested for potency to meet industry standards for purity. We carefully select manufacturing processes that yield and preserve the maximum ingredient potency. We avoid using excipients, fillers, and binding agents whenever possible and do not add artificial flavors or coloring to our products. We partner with herbalists, medical and naturopathic healthcare practitioners to develop potent, effective nutraceutical supplements that target our customers' health needs.

Formulating

We work with GMP (Good Manufacturing Practices) compliant manufacturers whenever possible. All vendors must meet rigorous manufacturing standards to ensure quality.

  • Our ingredients are tested for potency and meet industry standards for purity.
  • We carefully select manufacturing processes that yield and preserve the maximum ingredient potency.
  • We avoid using excipients, fillers, and binding agents whenever possible and do not add artificial flavors or coloring to our products.
  • We partner with herbalists, medical and naturopathic healthcare practitioners to develop potent, effective nutraceutical supplements that target our customers' health needs.

Sourcing

Most of our products are Made in the USA and globally sourced. All ingredients used in our products are meticulously researched and tested.

  • We partner with small domestic and international farmers whenever possible.
  • We continue to strive to use organic, Non-GMO, or wild-crafted ingredients whenever possible.

Testing

We believe in maintaining impeccable quality standards. Many of our products are tested throughout the formulation process. All products are tested for heavy metals and microbial contamination.

Testing Checklist

  • Nutrient Selection
  • Microbiological Organism Analysis
  • Heavy Metal Analysis
  • Potency Analysis
  • Residual Solvents Analysis
  • Physical/Chemical/Organoleptic Analysis
  • Cross Contamination Control
  • pH Testing
